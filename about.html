WHO WE ARE

The Chainsmokers is an American electronic DJ and production duo consisting of Alexander "Alex" Pall and Andrew "Drew" Taggart (with Matt McGuire on live shows). They started out by releasing remixes of songs by indie artists.


The EDM-pop duo achieved a breakthrough with their 2014 song "#Selfie", which became a top twenty single in several countries.

Alex Pall

Alex Pall was born on May 16, 1985 in New York City, New York, USA as Alexander Pall.
He is an actor and composer, known for Paris, Hardflip and The Chainsmokers Feat. Halsey: Closer - Lyric Video (2016).

He studied at New York University (2007) and The Masters School.
In the duo, Alex is the artistic director, actual DJ, and arranges all the collaborations.
During live shows, Alex does most of the dj-ing and mixing during the set as well as picking the songs to mix.
